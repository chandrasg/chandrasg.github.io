We leverage large-scale digital data for real-time public health monitoring and surveillance, partnering with state Departments of Health and the World Bank Group.

**Key areas:**
- COVID-19 discourse tracking and mental health monitoring during the pandemic
- Vaccine acceptance and hesitancy analysis across populations
- Substance use and opioid crisis monitoring
- Cardiovascular risk stratification from social media
- Population-level health behavior assessment

This work is supported by multiple NIH grants and has been implemented with partners including state health departments.
