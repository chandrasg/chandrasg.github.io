We study cultural variation in language, emotion expression, and health behaviors across diverse populations, with the goal of building AI systems that are culturally aware and equitable.

**Key areas:**
- Cross-cultural emoji usage and digital communication norms
- Politeness, stress, and emotional expression across Eastern and Western cultures
- Culturally-aware NLP for health applications in India, China, and sub-Saharan Africa
- Gender norms in global media, in collaboration with the World Bank Group
- Bias detection and mitigation in health AI models
