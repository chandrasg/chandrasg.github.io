We design and evaluate AI-powered conversational agents and digital health interventions that are scalable, culturally informed, and integrated with clinical workflows.

**Key areas:**
- Conversational agents for health behavior change
- Culturally-aware chatbots for mental health support
- Integration of patient-generated data into electronic health records
- Digital interventions for smoking cessation, medication adherence, and health literacy
- Smartphone-based health monitoring and ecological momentary assessment
